# Procurement And Civic Tech (Future Application)

ClaimBound can help independent verifiers, procurement teams and civic-tech
groups inspect **what is checkable** before adoption — not whether to award a
contract or certify a vendor.

## Suitable narrow claims

- A public procurement or product claim has a frozen source boundary.
- An official dataset page passed a source-audit gate.
- A blocked-source outcome is recorded when the manifest is incomplete.

## Unsuitable broad claims

- Legal, compliance or procurement approval.
- Vendor superiority or award recommendation.
- Deployment readiness from a source audit alone.
