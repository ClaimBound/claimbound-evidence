# Education And Open Science (Future Application)

ClaimBound can support teaching reproducible ML and open-science appendices
when claims stay narrow and protocols are frozen before runs.

## Suitable narrow claims

- A classroom exercise reproduced a fixed gate under documented commands.
- A paper appendix links to a compact evidence record with limitations.
- A negative or blocked outcome is preserved instead of hidden.

## Unsuitable broad claims

- Universal method superiority.
- Leaderboard ranking without a frozen scoring rule.
- Certification of student or publication quality.
