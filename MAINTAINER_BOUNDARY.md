# Maintainer Boundary

ClaimBound is reusable open-source evidence infrastructure, not an on-demand
review service.

## What The Maintainer Can Reasonably Do

- Maintain validators, schema docs, examples and registry structure.
- Review PRs that add bounded evidence cards or rerun records.
- Clarify claim boundaries and forbidden inferences.
- Improve source-boundary and rerun workflows.
- Tag releases and document known limitations.

## What The Maintainer Does Not Promise

- Running third-party benchmarks on demand.
- Certifying AI systems, models, datasets or vendors.
- Making award, procurement, legal, compliance or investment decisions.
- Hosting private data or restricted transcripts.
- Guaranteeing a result will be green.
- Guaranteeing turnaround time for evidence requests.

## Safe Request Shape

A good request has:

- one narrow public claim;
- a public source URL;
- a proposed protocol or checklist boundary;
- a scoring or resolution rule where applicable;
- a clear list of claims the card must not make.

Requests that are too broad should be split before any evidence work begins.
